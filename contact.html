Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get technical support for your products?

For technical support, please visit our support center where you'll find documentation, tutorials, and troubleshooting guides. If you can't find what you need, you can submit a support ticket through the portal or contact our support team directly at support@lumialabs.com.

What are your business hours?

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time. However, our support team monitors emails and tickets 24/7, with responses typically within 4-6 hours for urgent issues.

Do you offer enterprise solutions?

Yes! We provide customized enterprise solutions tailored to your organization's needs. Please contact our sales team at sales@lumialabs.com to discuss your requirements and schedule a consultation with one of our solutions architects.

Where can I find documentation for your APIs?

Comprehensive API documentation is available in our developer portal. You'll find detailed guides, code samples in multiple languages, and interactive API explorers to help you get started quickly with our platform.

How can I join your team?

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team! Check out our careers page for current openings. Even if you don't see a perfect match, we encourage you to submit your resume - we often create positions for exceptional candidates.
